UNDENIABLE CEO

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 July 2026 · App: Undeniable CEO (com.undeniable.ceo) · Developer: Yaroslav Gubanov (ThinkBigDoBigger), United Arab Emirates

The short version

Undeniable CEO is local-first. Your game progress lives on your device. An account is optional; if you create one, we store your email address and your game progress so you can sync across devices. We use analytics to understand how the game is used. We show no ads, and we never sell your data.

Information we collect

Email address (optional). Only if you choose to create an account (one-time code or Google sign-in), used for authentication and cross-device sync of your game progress. Accounts are provided through Supabase (privacy policy).

Usage data. Anonymous app interaction events (screens viewed, levels played, feature usage) and basic device information, collected through PostHog (privacy policy) to improve the game. If you sign in, these events may be associated with your account.

Game progress. Stored locally on your device; synced to our servers only if you create an account.

We collect no location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

Purchases

If in-app purchases are offered, payment is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never receive or store your card or payment details.

How we use information

To operate the game, sync your progress, understand usage, and improve the product. We do not sell or rent personal information, we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing, and we show no advertising. Our service providers (Supabase, PostHog, Google Play) process data only on our behalf.

Retention & deletion

Local data stays on your device until you delete the app or reset progress in Settings. Account data is retained while your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time — email us and we will delete it within 30 days.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you may withdraw consent for optional features (such as your account) at any time. Contact us at the address below.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Account data is stored with access controls (row-level security) so only you can read your progress.

Children

Undeniable CEO is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy on this page and update the effective date above.
