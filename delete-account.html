Delete your account and data

How to request deletion

  1. Email yaroslav.gubanov@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Undeniable CEO account".
  2. Send the request from the email address you used to sign in to the app (so we can verify it's you).
  3. We will delete your account and confirm by reply within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Everything associated with your account: your email address, your synced game progress, and any analytics events linked to your account identity. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

What is kept

Nothing tied to your identity. Aggregated, anonymised usage statistics (which cannot be linked back to you) may be retained.

Local data only?

If you played without an account, all data lives on your device — erase it in the app (Settings → Reset all progress) or by uninstalling.
